If you wish to opt-out of personalized ads, you can opt-out anytime through the app or your phone settings. Just follow these steps:

For Android Devices

1) Go to Phone Settings.
2) Tap on Google.
3) Select Ads.
4) Switch on "Opt-out of personalized ads".

For iOS Devices

1. Open the Settings app.
2. Tap Privacy.
3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and tap Advertising.
4. Tap Reset Advertising Identifier.
5. When prompted, tap Reset Advertising Identifier.
